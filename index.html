d-Drums is a music application for mobile devices intended to use for easy and quick creation of music with collaboration of your friends. The aplication would be able to detect what other people around you are playing and it will give visual feedback in your screen. You could even play along with guitars, drums or in desesparation you can hit whatever you want to make sounds!!! (just not your friends)

No need to be an expert musician to use this app, just touch the screen as you like. The applicaction will be easy configurable in seconds to change the sound drastically or you could use a desktop application for more technic manipulation.
